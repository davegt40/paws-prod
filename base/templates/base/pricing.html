Pricing

Dog Walking and Let Outs

Whether you have an unruly pet or your pet just needs to get out for a break, we’ve got you covered! A properly exercised dog is a happy dog, and happy dogs make for happy families! Paws Around Motown provides safe, private, on-leash exercise for your beloved pup! These range from 20 to 60 minutes, and always include fresh water, undivided attention, positive reinforcement, and a safe environment for your pet to expel their extra energy! Walk hours are between the hours of 8 am and 8 pm, but early and late walks can be added for a small fee.

Pricing
  • 20 Minutes - $18
  • 30 Minutes - $20
  • 45 Minutes - $26
  • 60 Minutes - $30
  • Add $5.50 if walk is before 8 am or after 8 pm
  • Add $5.50 for each additional dog if more than 2 dogs
  • On demand walks/visits may require an additional fee

Pet Sitting

Going out of town and prefer your pet stay in the comfort of their own home? Not all pets require an overnight companion. Paws Around Motown provides private vacation services that allow your pet to stay in their own safe and comfortable environment! We will come by multiple times per day (minimum of 3 per day for dogs, and once per day for cats) to provide your pet with individualized care! This service includes but is not limited to; bringing in the mail, bringing the trash cans in and out, litter box maintenance, medication administration, feedings, fresh water, and private exercise and play! Visit hours are between the hours of 8 am and 8 pm, but early and late visits can be added for a small fee.

Pricing
  • $24 per visit
  • This visit is 45 minutes in length. We require a minimum of three visits per day for dogs and one visit per day for cats
  • Add $5.50 if visit is before 8 am or after 8 pm
  • Add $5.50 for each additional dog and $3.50 for each additional cat if more than 2 dogs or cats

Overnight Pet Sitting

If you feel like your pet needs a bedtime companion, then overnight pet sitting is for you! This service is designed for pets who require the snuggles of a human to get through the night. We will provide 12 hours of overnight care for your beloved baby. This service also includes the same things as our pet sitting visits, but with the undivided attention of an overnight companion. Typically paired with at least one midday service, this can provide your pet with the normalcy of having their routines undisturbed! This is a custom quoted service and available on a first come, first serve basis!

Pricing
  • Call for a custom quote

Everything But The Slumber

This service is designed for pets that don’t necessarily need a sleeping companion, but would benefit from an extended period of time with their pet sitter before bedtime. With Everything but the Slumber, your pet(s) get 3 hours of undivided attention in the evening and all of the bells and whistles of an overnight pet sitting service, without someone sleeping in your home! We will return first thing in the morning for 1 hour to have breakfast and another private walk.

Pricing
  • $110 per sit
  • Add $5.50 for each additional dog and $3.50 for each additional cat if more than 2 dogs or cats

Basic Obedience Training

Have a certified dog trainer come to your home and work with you and your dog, on a weekly basis, to learn basic obedience cues: sit, down, loose leash walking, etc.

Pricing
  • $85 per session
  • Each session lasts approxmately 45 to 60 minutes

Lunch and Learn

Need assistance with the consistency and repetition of training? Have a certified trainer come and walk your dog at lunchtime while training on a foundation of proper and polite walking skills. A minimum of two sessions per week is required for this service with a weekly to bi-weekly Basic Obedience Training session booked for a trainer to work with the family. Space is limited.

Pricing
  • $55 per session
  • Each session lasts approxmately 30 to 45 minutes

Yard Waste Cleanup Service

Why spend time you don’t have cleaning up your pet’s waste, when we can do it for you? Treat yourself to weekly yard waste clean ups!

Pricing
  • Spring Cleanup/Initial Cleanup Service
    • Before we begin regular service we will do a first time clean up
    • One dog - $50
    • Two dogs - $65
    • Three or more dogs - $75
  • Regular Service
    • One time per week
    • One dog - $20
    • Two dogs - $26
    • Three or more dogs - $32

Pet Taxi

Transport pets and pet care supplies to and from boarding facility, home of a family member or friend, veterinarian, groomer, or other location.

Pricing
  • $25 per 30 minutes

Additional Fees

Last Minute Requests

If a service is requested less than 24 hours in advance, there will be an additional $10 surcharge.

Early and Late Walks/Visits

Normal visit hours are from 8 am until 8 pm. We will provide visits outside of our normal business hours for an additional $5.50 per walk/visit.

Cancellations

During non-holiday periods, cancellations must be received no later than 24 hours in advance. Less than 24-hour notice will result in clients being billed 50% of the cancelled service. Cancellation fees may be waived due to inclement weather conditions. Cancellations made the day of service, vacation visits, and during holiday weeks and peak vacation periods may not be refunded or credited.

Holiday Services

Additional $10.50 charge for each visit on the following holidays: New Years Day, Easter, Memorial Day, Fourth of July, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, New Years Eve
